## üìê Australian Child Support Formula

Based on official Department of Social Services guidelines:

1. **Adjusted Taxable Income** = Gross Income - Self-Support Amount ($29,841)
2. **Combined Income Calculation**
3. **Income Share Ratio**
4. **Child Support Assessment**
5. **Care Arrangement Adjustments**
6. **Final Child Support Amount**

### Key Thresholds
- **Self-Support Amount**: $29,841
- **Income Threshold**: $44,762
- **Support Rate**: 26% above threshold
- **New Estimate Rule**: 15% income reduction
